Claw Viper (Animal)
Tomb Viper, Claw Viper, Salamander, Pit Viper, Serpent Magus

The origin of these demonic monstrosities is cloaked in controversy. Whether they are the victims of a curse of a powerful sorceress or the result of some hell-spawned evolution, these beasts now serve in the armies of The Three. Those who choose to hunt these creatures should be warned that they are extremely fast and their touch has been known to sometimes chill their victims to the bone.

Tips and Other Additional Information
Claw Viper attacks can be chilling and losing your normal speed can be fatal in a pack of these creatures. Claw Vipers use Charge augmented with an elemental attack.
