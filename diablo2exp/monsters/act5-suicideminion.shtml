Suicide Minion
Fanatic Enslaved (Animal), Berserker Slayer (Animal), Consumed Ice Boar (Animal), Consumed Fire Boar (Animal), Frenzied Hell Spawn (Demon), Frenzied Ice Spawn (Animal), Insane Hell Spawn (Demon), Insane Ice Spawn (Animal)

The newest monsters created by Baal to serve in his army of destruction, they are stealthy, quick-footed, and unusually tough for their size. Bony knobs protrude from their thick hides, giving them added protection against melee weapons.

Tips and Other Additional Information
Suicide Minions are similar to Warcraft's Goblin Sappers. They attempt to charge you and blow up in your face doing significant damage, including a cold-chilling-damage explosion. Characters with low amounts of Life are often killed in one hit of a Suicide Minion. It's somewhat easy to fool these Suicide Minions by running around in circles until they blow up (like a Jet running from a heat seeking missile), or by "training" or leading the monster to another target such as your Hireling or another player in your party (*wink*). Amazons can use Decoys, other classes with Minions can lead Suicide Minions into their Minions. Suicide Minions deal no damage to Hirelings or Minions so you can exploit this to your advantage.

You don't get experience if they blow up. They do not drop loot, not even from barbarian's Find Item.
