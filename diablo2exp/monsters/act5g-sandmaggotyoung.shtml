Sand Maggot Young (Animal) (Guest Monster)
World Killer Young

While the adolescent Sand Maggot does not have the poisonous mucous of its parent, it is incredibly aggressive and dangerous. Driven by an incessant hunger, they seek out live food to devour as their rapidly growing bodies demand more energy. They often hunt in broods and swarm over their prey, making them especially dangerous when encountered near freshly-hatched nesting grounds.

Tips and Other Additional Information
Sand Maggot Young can easily surround you so it's important to kill the Sand Maggots as soon as possible to prevent them from creating more young.
