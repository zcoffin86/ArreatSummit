Blood Lord
Moon Lord, Night Lord, Blood Lord, Hell Lord, Death Lord

Blood Lords are raging, uncontrollable brutes and have the freedom to act independently from the other demonic troops on the battlefield. They are strictly melee fighters, but their devastating blows inflict both normal and magic damage.

Tips and Other Additional Information
Blood Lords use Frenzy.

Moon Lords have a chance to deal Fire and Cold damage.
Night Lords have a chance to deal Lightning damage.
Hell Lords have a chance to deal Fire damage.
Death Lords have a chance to deal Fire damage.

There are also different monsters called, "Blood Lord" and "Night Lord".
