The Items the Amazon chooses depend on her playing style. There are several ways to play an Amazon, such as a Bowazon, Javazon, Spearazon or a Tankazon (one-handed Weapon and Shield). It is always best to maximize Fire and Lightning Resistances and build up Life to a decent level (250-500+) so that Amazons can survive hits on Hell Difficulty. Although this page has suggestions about popular items, there is no perfect guide to the best items to wear. Generally, it is best to rate items against each other and select the ones that best help your character. This page mostly lists the Ultimate Items rather than listing all options available. Check out the Item Section for a more complete list of what is available.

Basic Magic Ability List

Damage
The higher your damage, then the faster monsters will die. Find weapons that do the most damage possible. Runes or jewels that are "Enhanced Damage" can also improve equipment and socketed items.

Defense
Defense is very important for Spearazons and Javazons, but less important for Bowazons.

% Mana Steal, % Life Steal and Cold Damage
Amazons typically do not raise their energy. Instead, Amazons usually rely on % Mana Steal items to refill their Mana. Items that add to Mana will be sufficient to raise Mana reserves to a high enough level to support skills such as Decoy, Valkyrie and other spells. Mana Steal is also important to recover the cost of casting Valkyries and Decoys. % Life Steal keeps characters at full health and allows them to quickly recover from damage and use few potions. Cold Damage is also very useful because it not only chills monsters, but it also causes some of them to shatter, leaving no corpse for the monsters that are able to raise their fallen friends. One point of Cold Damage is all it takes to chill monsters. Popular items for Cold Damage are the Eye of Etlich and Frostburns. You can also use a Charm, which might be even easier, and allows you to wear an item with better stats than just + cold damage.

Sockets
Use the Socket Quest and Horadric Cube Formula to socket items (even Rare, Crafted, Set, Unique) and enhance them with good jewels and runes.

Resistances
It is best to try to maximize Resistances, especially Lightning, Fire and Cold.

Hit Recovery
Items with Hit Recovery allow you to quickly recover after having been hit. Without Hit Recovery items, when a character is hit, it will be stunned and will simply stand in place until able to move again. If multiple monsters are attacking at once, the character may remain continually stunned until it dies. But, with Hit Recovery items characters can quickly return to battle after being hit. Hit Recovery items stack, so they can be used on several different pieces of equipment (belts, armor, boots or, shields) significantly reducing the length of time a character remains stunned.

Charms
Good Charms to have are Cold Damage, + Life, + Skills, + Maximum Damage, + Elemental Damage, and + Resistances.

Weapons
Bowazons
The most important equipment to the Bowazon is, of course, her bow. When looking for weapons, damage is the main concern. Take Maximum (average) damage over any other magical bonus on the bow. Another important bonus is bow speed. Look for Increased Attack Speed bows or socket your bow with a Shael Rune (20% Increased Attack Speed). It is possible to maximize attack speed so characters can use bows without Increased Attack Speed while still having maximum Attack Speed. Other things that are nice are Elemental Damage (which can be resisted), % Mana/Life Steal, and +1-2 to Amazon Skills or +1-3 to Amazon Skill Tabs. Any other useful stats are a bonus.

Try to find the ultimate weapon of each weapon class. When evaluating weapons, keep the weapon that does the most average damage [(Minimum + Maximum)/ 2], rather than the the most maximum damage.

"Cruel" Magical Weapons, which can offer +201-300% damage, are available from vendors. Keep exiting and re-entering town to "re roll" the vendors until they offer one of these items. You can then socket the items and add other runes or jewels to them.

Use Imbue Quest Reward and Cube Formulas to attempt to create a high damage Rare or Crafted Weapon.

Generally, look for a higher % Blocking instead of Defense, and then add sockets, for runes or jewels, to shields for even more benefits.

Boots
Check out the Basic Magic Ability List for a list of magical bonuses to look for. Look for Boots that offer Defense and useful magical bonuses such as Faster Run/Walk, Fastest Hit Recovery, Resistances or additional Life/Mana (if needed). Boots that give a Faster Run/Walk Speed, and faster Stamina Regeneration can really help characters avoid danger and reach their destination much quicker.

Initially, look for boots that reduce Stamina drain or offer additional Stamina. Boots that Heal Stamina 50% will heal Stamina faster when characters stop. Vidala's Fetlock will give 150 additional Stamina. Treads of Cthon will take 50% less Stamina when characters run and walk. Which means, in essence, that it doubles Stamina. If Stamina doubled is greater than Stamina +150, then Treads of Cthon will allow a character to run longer than Vidala's.

As Characters accrue more vitality, they can eventually run as often as needed (within reason) and not run out of Stamina. At that point, Stamina reducing boots are no longer needed and should be replaced with "Fastest Run/Walk." This will free up Magical Prefix/Suffixes on your boots.
