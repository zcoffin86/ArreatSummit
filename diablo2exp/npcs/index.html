Interacting With Townsfolk (NPCs)
Interacting with townsfolk is important in Diablo II. They tell you about relevant events and people in the world of Sanctuary, and some offer goods or services to assist you in your adventures. Urgent News - An NPC with an exclamation point (!) overhead has urgent news for you. Usually, the news is important information about completing your quests and advancing in the game. You should approach and talk to any townsfolk with an "!" overhead.
